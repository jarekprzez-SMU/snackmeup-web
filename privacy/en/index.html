Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Version 1.0

Your privacy matters to us. This document explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Data Controller

Jarosław Przezdziecki
Poland
📧 support@snackmeup.com

As the data controller, we process your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Polish data protection law.

Data We Collect

Category Examples Purpose
Account data Email address, display name (optional), login provider (Google / Apple / email) Authentication, account management
Profile data Location (country, city), food allergies, kitchen equipment, cuisine preferences, difficulty level, budget AI recipe personalisation
Usage history Generated recipes, ratings, favourites, cooking history Anti-Monotony Engine, AI quality improvement
Subscription data Subscription status, renewal dates (via RevenueCat) Managing access to premium features
Technical data Device identifier, operating system, app version Error diagnostics, security
Analytics data (planned) Usage events, session duration, navigation flows UX improvement, retention analysis

Behavioural analytics data may be collected in future versions of the app using tools such as Sentry or similar analytics platforms. We will notify you before implementing such tracking.

How We Use Your Data

  • AI recipe generation: Your preferences, allergies, and location are sent to the Google Gemini API to generate personalised recipes. This data is not stored by Google in a way that identifies individual users, in accordance with Google Cloud terms.
  • Personalisation: Your cooking history is used to avoid repeating recipes (Anti-Monotony Engine).
  • Subscriptions: Subscription status is synchronised with RevenueCat to manage access to premium features.
  • Security: Technical data helps us detect and fix app errors.

Personal data is shared with third parties only to the extent necessary to provide the service. We do not sell your personal data.

Sensitive Data

Food allergies and dietary restrictions (e.g. Vegan, Keto) constitute health-related data under the GDPR. We collect this data solely:

  • based on your explicit consent given during profile setup,
  • to protect your safety during recipe generation,
  • exclusively within your account — we do not profile or share this data with advertisers.

Data Retention

  • Account and profile data: retained for the duration of your account.
  • Recipe history: retained for the duration of your account.
  • After account deletion: all data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Technical logs: deleted after 90 days.

Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data via your profile settings.
  • Erasure — delete your account and all data from within the app.
  • Portability — request an export of your data in JSON format.
  • Objection — object to processing of your data for analytics purposes.
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for processing sensitive data at any time.

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at: support@snackmeup.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. In Poland: Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO), uodo.gov.pl.

Advertising

Snack Me Up does not display ads and does not share data with advertisers. The app is funded solely through user subscriptions.

Children

Snack Me Up is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age. If you believe a child under 13 is using the app, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this Privacy Policy via an in-app notification or email. The date of the last update is always shown at the top of this document.
